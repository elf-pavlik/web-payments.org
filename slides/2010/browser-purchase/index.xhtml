A Browser-based PaySwarm Purchase

  • Acquires a License from a trusted source (Content Owner via PaySwarm Authority)
  • Processes payment through a trusted source (PaySwarm Authority)
  • Acquires digital content from people that have already purchased the same file. (Sellers)
  • Does all of this in a way that is transparent to the customer. (Browser integration)

Browser-based PaySwarm Purchase Steps

  1. Buyer finds a music album that they want to buy on a music blog.
  2. Buyer clicks "Buy Album" button and is presented with a price and digital contract details. Buyer reviews details.
  3. Buyer clicks "Purchase".
  4. Small download progress bar appears and notifies Buyer when the download is complete.
  • It sounds simple because it is simple. PaySwarm makes for-profit swarming digital content distribution as easy as downloading a file from the Web.
