The Goal of Website-based PaySwarm

  • Provide a purely server-based way to use PaySwarm.
  • Websites can implement PaySwarm independently of the browser manufacturers.
  • Easy and familiar to developers who have worked with OAuth (Twitter, Facebook, etc.)
  • Useful if a publisher only needs to sell licenses to access content. For example, if a publisher wants to sell access to journal articles and doesn't have expensive HD video data streaming requirements.
