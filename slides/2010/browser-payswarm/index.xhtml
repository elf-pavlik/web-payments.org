The Goal of Browser-based PaySwarm

  • Native, in-browser, copyright-aware, legal peer-to-peer digital content distribution.
  • Useful when content creators need to distribute massive amounts of high-definition content, such as video.
  • Reward content creators for creating awesome stuff and fans for helping to distribute the awesome stuff. Pay fans directly for helping to distribute content!
