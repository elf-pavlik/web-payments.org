What is the Web Payments CG?

  • Pre-standards payment technology community

  • 184 members, international and cross-domain

  • Incubator for tech w/ standards-potential (unofficial)

  • Open, inclusive, and transparent

  • Discussion, Use Cases, Requirements, Specs

Brief

History

2010-2014 Web Payments History


  • 2010 - Technology initiative started
  • 2011 - Web Payments CG Founded
  • 2013 - UN IGF - Trade, Taxation, Routing Money
  • 2013 - W3C TPAC - Building org. support
  • 2014 - W3C Web Payments Workshop
  • 2014 - UN IGF Workshop on Web Payments
  • 2014 - W3C TPAC first Web Payments F2F

What's

Broken

The Web Platform's current failures


  • Credentials - no standardized, verifiable 3rd party credentials

  • Payment Initiation - no standard way to initiate payments

  • Digital Receipts - no standard proof-of-purchase mechanism

Why

Care?

Why is identity and payment important?

  • Civic - Strong identity is central to ownership, democracy, privacy, and prosperity.

  • Moral - 2.5 billion adults don't have access to any financial infrastructure. Inequity.

  • Economic - 10 seconds to send an email, 172,800 seconds to send money. The Internet as financial infrastructure will lead to a more agile and vibrant global economy.
